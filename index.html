The client

Pohoda is the most famous Slovak music and arts festival with international acclaim, where all types of music meet various types of art and which, during a couple of days in July, becomes an "island of comfort" for 30.000 attendees.

The challenge

Our task was to create a mobile app that thousands of users could use during a short period of time. The biggest challenge was connecting the app with Pohoda's back office, with no option to test the app out before the event.

The solution

We created a useful mobile app that helped make the festival experience for its attendees the best. With the app, they could find and track their friends on a map, create custom playlists, discover food preferences and get important notifications.
